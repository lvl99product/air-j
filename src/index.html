How to sync across devices:

Air-J is a privacy-first, offline application. Your data never leaves your browser automatically. To use Air-J across multiple devices:

  1. Click Export Backup to download your tasks.
  2. Save this .json file to your preferred cloud drive:
    Google Drive • Dropbox • OneDrive
  3. Open Air-J on your other device and Import the file.
